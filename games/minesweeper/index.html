Minesweeper

I Can't Believe It's Not Minesweeper

1.3

From the murky depths of the 2016 projects directory comes a fresh new take on the classic PC puzzle game.
Completely rebuilt in 2018 with actually decent code to provide a much more polished and feature-rich experience.

Features:

  • Online co-operative multiplayer! Destroying relationships has never been this easy.
  • Automatically saves your game to resume at a later time. But why would you want to stop playing...?
  • Preset or custom difficulties, so your ride can be as easy or menacing as you wish.
  • NEW FOR 2018! Fancy sound and visual effects to make the game feel fuller than it actually is.

Android port coming soon! (it's already done, just can't release yet)
