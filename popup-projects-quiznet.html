QuizNet

A fast & simple homework distribution system for schools.

QuizNet is a networked homework system I built in 2018 for my A-Level Computer Science project.

It features a PHP webserver to handle the database, which stores data in a custom-written hierarchial CSV format. Cause I was too cool for SQL.

Secondly, a C# WinForms application is used by teachers to manage students & classes, create quizzes, assign them to classes, and analyse results. While less a e s t h e t i c than the other UI frameworks used, WinForms was chosen to feel familiar and simple to teachers already accustomed to management software like Sims.NET.

Finally, a Unity game is used for the students' Client, allowing them to see their assignments and play quizzes. In contrast to the WinForms client, this 'UI engine' was definitely over-the-top, but both were a case of taking advantage of prior experience with the tools.

Features:

  • Unlimited students, classes, quizzes & assignments (until the QuizNet admins invent pricing tiers)
  • Custom Quiz creator with extensive configuration options (time limits, random question selection, time limits, images etc)
  • Set deadlines for your assigned homework, to scare students with red boxes instead of blue ones
  • Logging and analytics of student performance
  • Secure storage of teacher logins & high database integrity until you put a comma in the wrong place
